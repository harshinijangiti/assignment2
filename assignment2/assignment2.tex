\section*{Question 26}
Candidates were asked to come to an interview with 3 pens each. Black, blue, green and red were
the permitted pen colours that the candidate could bring. The probability that a candidate comes
with all 3 pens having the same colour is

\section*{Solution}
Let $X_{i}$ be Random Variable represents a pen
\newline
\{$X_{1}$,$X_{2}$,$X_{3}$\} represents 3 pens
\newline
Black, blue, green, red colour pens represents $X_i$\in\{0,1,2,3\}
\newline
Probability of all 3 pens having same color=

\[
\Rightarrow \frac{\text{No of Outcomes with 3 pens having same colour}}{\text{Total No of outcomes}}
\]
\begin{align}
\Rightarrow\frac{n(\sum_{k=0}^{3}X_1=X_2=X_3=k)}{n(\sum_{k=0}^{3}X_1=k)\cdot n(\sum_{k=0}^{3}X_2=k)\cdot n(\sum_{k=0}^{3}X_3=k)}
\end{align}
\begin{align}
\Rightarrow Pr(X_1=X_2=X_3)=\frac{4}{4\cdot4\cdot4}
\end{align}
\begin{align}
\Rightarrow0.06
\end{align}
